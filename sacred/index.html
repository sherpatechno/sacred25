Who We Are

At Sacred Himalaya, we believe that travel is more than just visiting places - it's about immersing yourself in cultures, connecting with people, and creating lifelong memories.

Founded by Carlo, an experienced traveler and cultural enthusiast, our company specializes in unique journeys to some of the world's most fascinating destinations.

Our focus on the Omo Valley stems from our passion for preserving and sharing ancient cultures that are rapidly changing in our modern world.

We work closely with local communities to ensure our travels are respectful, sustainable, and beneficial to the people who welcome us into their homes.

Why With Us?

Nepal is where everything began for me and my second home. It's a lifelong destination for many. I owe to the country and its people the motivations that drove me to travel for a living, to the point of making it my profession.

With Sacred Himalaya specialized in Nepal, as a travel and life destination: I also dedicated the Finale for Nepal project to the people, their history, and their culture, which must be protected and supported.

- Carlo, Founder

LIVING THE JOURNEY

I consider myself a world explorer.

For me, traveling means moving, with a free and respectful spirit, to encounter places and people. It means feeling enriched by the lands I've encountered, the challenges I've faced, the goals I've achieved, and the dreams I've fulfilled. There's too much beauty to keep to myself. Whether it's Asia, Africa, Europe, or South America, I'm ready, along with my group of companions, to travel with you in a safe and organized manner.

And it won't just be a journey but an encounter: with others, with the world, with yourself.

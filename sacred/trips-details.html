EGYPT, PHARAOHS AND DESERT

Egypt, land of the Pharaohs. Cradle of civilization and guardian of history's best-kept secrets. It will forever be associated with the pyramids, the Great Sphinx, and the Valley of the Kings, but Egypt's allure extends far beyond the archaeological mysteries still being uncovered beneath its sands. It is a country of profound charm, intriguing culture, and countless stories: a trip to Egypt offers an unforgettable experience that will last a lifetime.

On one side are the Pyramids of Giza, the temples of Luxor and Karnak, and the Valley of the Kings. You'll see centuries of history unfold before you. You'll follow in the footsteps of the pharaohs, from some of the world's oldest pyramids to the ancient tombs of the pharaohs, along a fascinating cradle of over 5,000 years of history and culture.

On the other, the oases of Bahariya, Dakhla, and Farafra, with the spectacular White Desert. Prepare to sleep under the stars on what was once an ocean floor millions of years ago, guarded by towering white sentinels, sculpted by sand and wind. This is Egypt like you've never seen before.

3 MEMORABLE MOMENTS ON A TRIP TO EGYPT

  1. Count the stars in the company of the rock formations of the White Desert.
  2. Be amazed by the grandeur of the Sphinx and the Pyramids of Giza.
  3. Take a step back in time among the temples and tombs of Luxor.

Day 1: Italy / Entebbe

Flight and departure day: it's the start of our gorilla tour in Uganda. We meet at the airport. Head to Entebbe.

Overnight stay : on board the flight.

Day 2: Entebbe – Masindi (5 hours – excluding stops)

We arrive very early and head directly to the Airport Guest House (or similar). After freshening up, we meet our guides and prepare for the first leg of the trip. In five hours, we reach the Ziwa Game Reserve to spot rhinos on our first safari. We then continue to Masindi, where we stay for the night.

Meals included : breakfast, lunch, and dinner.

Overnight stay : Masindi Hotel (or similar).

Day 3: Masindi – Murchison Falls (2 hours – excluding stops)

The day begins in the direction of the largest of Uganda's National Parks: Murchison Falls. We cross the Budongo forest (rich in primates and ancient mahogany trees) for a trek in search of chimpanzees before reaching the “Top of the Falls,” the summit of Murchison Falls, where the Nile narrows before making a spectacular 43m drop.

Meals included : breakfast, lunch and dinner.

Overnight stay : Bamboo Village (or similar).

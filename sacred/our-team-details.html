ALESSANDRO ODDERA

I was born in Savona, my name is Alino, and I have been working with Viaggia con Carlo for a long time.

I spent my youth among the peaks of the Maritime Alps, traversing them with my sealskins, before embarking on the wildest and most beautiful descents in Italy. I can vouch for my ability to be an off-piste skier and, in general, a true outdoor expert. I lead groups mostly in Nepal and Latin America, where I gained experience in Patagonia, Peru, Bolivia, and, of course, Nepal.

I like to approach every adventure with a positive and cheerful spirit: experiencing adventure safely, even in large groups, is the key to an enriching experience.

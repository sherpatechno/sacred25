CHARLES

CEO / Founder

Traveler, explorer, lover, and outdoor expert : this is how I describe myself. I was born into a family that has always been involved in travel, but before I could do so myself and find my own path, I decided to backpack the world : Latin America, for seven long years, India, Southeast Asia, until I reached Nepal, my second home, which I explored in its entirety via the main trekking routes, from the classics to the most remote.

Becoming an expert on the destination and finding my life's direction began here, and continued thanks to meeting my future partners at Himalayan Trailfinder. With Arun Gurung and Shanu Rana, I founded the Nepalese tour operator "Viaggiaconcarlo by Himalayan Trailfinder", specializing in trekking, expeditions, and tours in Nepal and Tibet, and then I founded Viaggia con Carlo. Adventure, hiking, and mountaineering trips are at the heart of my work as a tour operator, but I have always tried to view travel as a human experience, supporting the people of the countries I visit.

This is why I founded, together with some friends, the association "Finale for Nepal", which gives its name to one of the largest Italian outdoor festivals. My life passes not only through the mountains and the Himalayan peaks, but also through the sea and sailing, a great passion and profession that I follow directly.
